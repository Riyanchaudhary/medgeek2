BMI Calculator

BMI Insights:

  • Underweight: BMI less than 18.5. Consider consulting a doctor or nutritionist.
  • Normal weight: BMI between 18.5 and 24.9. Maintain a healthy lifestyle!
  • Overweight: BMI between 25 and 29.9. Consider lifestyle changes to improve health.
  • Obese: BMI of 30 or greater. Consult a doctor to discuss health risks and management.
